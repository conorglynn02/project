About Me


Hello, my name is Conor Glynn and I am 19 years old. I just finished my Leaving Cert exams a few months ago in June 2021. I went to school in St. Jarlath’s College, Tuam where I studied eight subjects. These include the core subjects (English, Irish and Maths), three science subjects; biology, chemistry and physics along with applied maths and DCG. My favourite subject in school was definately DCG as I feel that is where I could show off my ideas and creativity. I was recognised for my high academic achievement in the Leaving Certificate where I was in the top 5% of results in the country. I was awarded for receiving amongst the highest Leaving Cert points in my school.


I am the youngest of four and the son of two extremely hard-working parents. My Dad is a part-time farmer and window-fitter. My Mom is a factory worker. I aspire to be as hard-working and caring as they are.


I took a gap year before deciding to go to university. I took this gap year because I wanted to explore my options for the future and what my interests are. During my year away from secondary education, I learned that I really enjoy creating things and technology really peaks my interest. I created this website as a challenge for myself and also to help my father who helps me everyday without hesitation.
