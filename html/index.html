Product Design

I designed this project for my Leaving Cert DCG (Design Communication Graphics) portfolio 2021. In this project, I had to create a dash-cam which fitted a certain theme. The theme I chose was Arsenal F.C. as it is my favourite club. I feel that my dash-cam would be an excellent product to be sold as official Arsenal merchandise.

Planning

I sketched some different ideas on what the dash-cam would look like. I had to think about its functionality and usability while also keeping with the theme of Arsenal. I came up with a number of original concepts and I worked out the pros and the cons of each one before I made my final decision.

Computer Aided Design (CAD)

I used CAD to create possible finished products in Solidworks. By doing this, I could see what my products would look like in real life with renders and some editing. I really enjoyed working with Solidworks as I feel like I could bring out my creative side with it.
